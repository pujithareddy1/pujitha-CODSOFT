OCEAN

THE GIVER OF LIFE

Water is one of the most familiar and ubiquious substances on Earth,with an estimated total volume of some 1386 million cubic kilometers.Of this more than 97%, covers over 70% of the planet's surface in the form of our oceans,while the remaining three percent of freshwater is held mainly in glaciers and the icecaps(69%),in groundwater in the subterranean lithosphere(30%),in terrestrial surface waters such as swamps,rivers and lakes(0.03%) and in our atmosphere as water vapour,which is constantly being transporred and recycled by the hydrological cycle.

There are so many topics related to oceans such as ocean formation,continental drift,topography of the ocean floor,mid-ocean ridges,changing sea levels,costal landscapes,Deep water circulation,currents and climate control,polar ice sheets,the ocean edge,coral reef... etc.
